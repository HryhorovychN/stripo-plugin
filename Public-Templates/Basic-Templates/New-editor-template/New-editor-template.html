New features coming soon

Dark mode for UI

See the emails with the eyes of those users who prefer the Dark Mode on their devices by switching the editor Preview mode from the Dark mode to the Light mode and back. Stop wasting time on sending lots of test emails.

Co-editing

Working on emails with your teammates simultaneously is not a dream anymore — you can all edit emails at the same time. You see the changes right away. Most importantly, users have the roles and access levels that you have previously assigned to them.

Commenting in emails

Clients and colleagues will be able to leave comments right in emails. Just like in Google Docs, you share what you like and dislike about certain elements. With this option, the entire approval process will be as easy as a walk in the park.

Spell checkers

With the new editor, you don’t have to worry about spelling mistakes — we’re on it. No need to copy email texts and run them with third-party tools. Stripo will have spell checkers for multiple languages embedded in the editor.
